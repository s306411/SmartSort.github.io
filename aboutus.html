About us

SmartSort is a new and exciting company that works within climate policy throughout the world. New climate change will create an unrecognizable world. Therefore, we must make an effort to help stop greenhouse gas emissions. The company's goal is to make source sorting easier, so that you can make an effort to help save our planet. Our new and fresh product of a rubbish bin will do you a great service in everyday life.

Our mission

Our mission is to combine source sorting and technology, to make it easier for you to sort. By sorting at source, greenhouse gas emissions will be reduced, and thus climate change may also be repaired.
