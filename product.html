Our product

The product will be a fairly normal rubbish bin, but with a slight twist. In the rubbish bin, a camera will be placed to take a picture of what you are going to throw away. Once the camera has detected the product, the correct trash can will light up so you know exactly where to throw it. This way, source sorting will be a breeze, and you've done something good for our planet! Materials to be used for this product include metal, hard plastic, in addition to us using a camera / scanner, lights with bulbs and a sensor.

Our product is aimed at all user groups. Since everyone throws rubbish, we want to make it easier for everyone to sort. More specifically, our product is aimed at, for example, those who have problems and difficulties with source sorting (for example children), and those who are lazy and have less time.
